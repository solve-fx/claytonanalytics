About Us

Using your data effectively

Founded in early 2019, Clayton Analytics is a data science consultancy specialising in using machine learning to extract actionable insight from your data.

Machine learning can be used to quickly and accurately identify relationships in your data, allowing you to understand which factors are driving business performance, and how to optimise them.

Our services can be offered with a flexibility that suits you, from a few hours to a permanent addition to your team.
